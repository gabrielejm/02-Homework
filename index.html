About Me

Hello, my name is Justin Gabriele! I am currently in the service industry (bartending specifically), and have also worked in construction/carpentry in my past. Now, I am working to start a fulfulling and lucrative career in web development. I love solving problems and creating something I feel is useful, and I love the feelings associated with accomplishing that. Someday, I would like to be a developer for a big tech company. The great takeaway from working in the service industry is that my soft skills have greatly improved and even excelled. I'm great at making friends and getting along with anyone, as well as dealing with unhappy or upset customers in a productive and effective way. I know I have a lot to learn and am always open to criticism!

In my free time I enjoy gaming, bodybuilding, attending conventions and festivals, cosplaying, and going out for a drink or two. I love animals and have several pets including 2 cats, a chameleon, a leopard gecko, and a red-tailed boa constrictor.
